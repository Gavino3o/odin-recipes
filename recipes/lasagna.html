Vegetarian Spinach Lasagna

Description

Special homemade, truly fine and wicked lasagna to fill your bellies in the upcoming Christmas holidays.

Recipe taken from this webpage. Big thanks to Mottsbela.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 9 sheets of lasagna noodles
  • 1 bunch of fresh spinach
  • 230g ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch dried basil
  • Salt and grounded black pepper
  • 1 cup tomato pasta sauce
  • ½ cup of shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200℃.
  2. Spray a casserole dish with cooking spray.
  3. Bring a large pot of generously salted water to boil. Cook lasagna in boiling water for the duration recommended on the box. Stir occasionally until it is cooked to al dente.
  4. Drain the pasta and shock it with ice water to prevent it from further cooking.
  5. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill the saucepan with water to just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil and add spinach. Cover the saucepan and steam for 2 to 6 minutes until tender. Drain the spinach
  6. Mix steamed spinach, ricotta cheese, ½ cup Mozzarella cheese, lightly beated egg, nutmeg, basil, salt and grounded black pepper in a mixing bowl until throughly and evenly combined.
  7. Spread ¼ cup tomato pasta sauce on the bottom of the prepared casserole dish; top with 3 sheets of lasagna noodles, ½ cup of the ricotta mixture, and ¼ cup tomato pasta sauce. Repeat layers of 3 more noodles, ½ cup ricotta mixture, and ¼ cup pasta sauce.
    • End with remaining 3 lasagna noodles and ¼ cup pasta sauce. Sprinkle ½ cup Mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese on top. Fully cover casserole with aluminum foil.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Uncover the aluminium foil from the casserole and continue baking for roughly another 25 minutes until lasagna is bubbling and golden browned.
  9. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
